About Me

I am a data analyst and product analyst working with startups and growing businesses to turn messy data into decisions that actually move the needle. Before data, I spent years as a retail manager responsible for sales performance, inventory planning, and customer purchasing behavior. That experience matters more than most analytics certificates. I have lived inside operational problems, revenue pressure, and customer churn. Data wasn't abstract to me. It was survival.

Today, I apply that business-first mindset to early-stage and scaling products. I have worked as a founding data analyst for a digital menu product in the restaurant space, supported growth and engagement analysis for a subscription-sharing platform, and delivered analytical projects across product, marketing, and operations.

I am especially interested in product analytics, user behavior, growth metrics, and operational insights for startups and SMEs that need clarity, not complexity. If you are building something and your data feels noisy, underused, or ignored, that's where I do my best work.
